EXTCON FOR PALMAS/TWL CHIPS

PALMAS USB COMPARATOR
Required Properties:
 - compatible : Should be "ti,palmas-usb" or "ti,twl6035-usb"

Optional Properties:
 - ti,wakeup : To enable the wakeup comparator in probe

palmas-usb {
       compatible = "ti,twl6035-usb", "ti,palmas-usb";
       ti,wakeup;
};
